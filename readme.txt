== Description ==

Reviews lets you collect customer reviews, place on your site with shortcodes, and approve before asking for third-party reviews (such as Google or Yelp).


= Key Features =

* Collect customer reviews
* Place reviews on your site with shortcodes
* Approve reviews before asking the customer to submit third-party reviews (such as Google or Yelp)

== Installation ==

1. Download the plugin

2. Extract the contents of rera-reviews.zip to wp-content/plugins/ folder. You should get a folder called rera-reviews.

3. Activate the Plugin in WP-Admin.

4. Goto Settings > Reviews to configure
